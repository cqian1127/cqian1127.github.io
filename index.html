Welcome to my homepage!

 

I joined IQVIA Inc., Cambridge, this September as a senior machine learning scientist. I'm working on healthcare related topics such as trial success prediction, adverse event prediciton, drug response prediction, etc. My research interests include machine learning, signal processing, matrix/tensor factorization and optimization.

Before that, I was working with Prof. Nikos Sidiropoulos for about five years since 2014. I first joined Prof. Sidiropoulos' STAR group in Oct. 2014 as a visiting Ph.D. student at Dept. of ECE, University of Minnesota Twin Cities, and later on became a postdoc after my graduation in Jan. 2017.

I received the B. E. degree in communication engineering from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2011, and M. E. and Ph.D. degrees in information and communication engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China, in 2013 and 2017, respectively.
