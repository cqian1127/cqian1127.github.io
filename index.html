Welcome to my homepage!

 

I'm a Postdoctoral Associate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Virginia, under the supervision of Prof. Nikos Sidiropoulos.

I received the B. E. degree in communication engineering from Hangzhou Dianzi University, Hangzhou, China, in 2011, and M. E. and Ph.D. degrees in information and communication engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China, in 2013 and 2017, respectively. During Oct. 2014 - Sep. 2016, I was a joint Ph.D. student at University of Minnesota Twin Cities, where I was a postdoc before moving to Virginia, working with Prof. Sidiropoulos. My research interests include tensor factorization, signal processing and machine learning.
